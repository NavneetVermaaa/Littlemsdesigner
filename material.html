Material Details

01 Venetian Plaster Paint

Luxurious textured marble-like wall finish

  • Raw Cost:₹180 – ₹350 / sq.ft
  • Thickness:1–3 mm finished texture coat
  • Install:Prep wall → Base → Texture/Venetian → Seal
  • Maint:Wipe dust lightly, reseal every few years
  • Best Places:Living room, bedroom accent walls, hotel lobbies, luxury bathrooms, foyer, reception
  • Brands:Vasari India
✓ Pros

Creates luxurious depth like stone/polished plaster. Can be customized with colour, sheen, vein patterns.

✗ Cons

Requires skilled applicator. Not ideal for high-moisture areas without proper sealing.

02 Marble-Finish Tiles

Elegant marble-look durable floor tiles

  • Raw Cost:₹90 / sq.ft onwards
  • Thickness:8–10 mm tile thickness
  • Install:Lay tiles on pre-prepared screed with adhesive → grout joints → seal if matte finish
  • Maint:Mop with mild detergent; avoid harsh acids
  • Best Places:Living room floors, bathroom walls, kitchen backsplash
  • Brands:Kajaria, Somany, Nitco
✓ Pros

Realistic marble aesthetic at fraction of stone cost. Durable for floors/walls, many vein styles.

✗ Cons

Can feel cold without rugs. Grout lines visible unless large format used.

03 Premium Finish Laminates

High-finish decorative laminates

  • Raw Cost:₹200 / sq.ft onwards
  • Thickness:0.8 mm – 1.5 mm
  • Install:Bond laminate sheets to ply/MDF with adhesive → edge banding
  • Maint:Wipe with soft damp cloth
  • Best Places:TV units, wardrobes, kitchen cabinets, panels
  • Brands:Royale Touche Luxury Laminates
✓ Pros

Wide range of textures (stone, wood, gloss, matte). Durable and easy to clean.

✗ Cons

Looks less rich than real wood. Can peel if exposed to moisture.
